Krishika

This is section where I will be talking about Krishika. So a little you have to know. Krishika is the mix of the names Krish and Rishika. When you want to say that two people like each other (as a joke or not) you sometimes make one name out of the names of the two people who are "together". This is called a ship name. So Krishika is the ship name for Krish and Rishika.
